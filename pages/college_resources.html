US College Application Guide

Test Categories:

  • Standardized Tests: ACT (scored out of 36), SAT (1600 max, aim for 1400+).
  • Language Tests: TOEFL (aim for 100+/120) or IELTS or Duolingo

International students are strongly encouraged to take these tests.

  • Submit certified transcripts for the last 4 years of secondary/high school.
  • Key Notes:
    • Uploaded by your college counselor (often a teacher or academic advisor).
    • Counselor acts as liaison between you and colleges.
  • Purpose: Showcase your personality and experiences.
  • Resources:
    • Common App Essay Prompts
    • College Essay Guy (Tips)
  • Require 2+ letters (1 counselor, 2 teachers from different subjects).
  • Pro Tip: Request letters from STEM and humanities teachers.
  • Scheduled post-application submission (often via Skype/Zoom).
  • Tips:
    • Be authentic and detailed in responses.
    • Dress neatly and arrive early.
  • Submit via CSS Profile.
  • Required Documents:
    • 2-year tax forms or employer income letters.
    • Bank statements for self-employed parents/guardians.
